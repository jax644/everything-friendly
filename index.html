Vegan Avocado Toast

This recipe is soooooo good

Prep Time:

  • Total: 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes>

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat bread
  • 1/2 tsp Onion powder
  • Juice from half of a lemon
  • Sesame Seeds for garnish
  • 1 medium avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread until crispy.
  2. While the bread is toasting, slice or mash half of an avocado, depending on your preference.
  3. Slice the cherry tomatoes into quarters
  4. When the toast is ready, spread the avocado onto the toast
  5. Top with cherry tomatoes, sesame seeds, garlic powder, and onion powder
  6. Squeeze the lemon juice on top
  7. Serve and enjoy!
